# User Personas

## Primary Personas

### The Organizer (Alex)

**Profile:**
- 45, avid golfer, plays twice weekly
- Organizes monthly outings with 12-16 players
- Currently uses spreadsheets and group texts

**Goals:**
- Easy event setup and member invites
- Automated leaderboard
- Simple game setup and settlement

**Pain Points:**
- Spreadsheets are error-prone
- Collecting money is awkward
- Keeping track of presses is chaotic

**Press! Solution:**
- Quick event creation
- Invite links for members
- Built-in games with press tracking
- Clear settlement display

### The Competitor (Blake)

**Profile:**
- 32, low handicap, competitive
- Plays in a regular Saturday group
- Loves match play and side games

**Goals:**
- Track performance against buddies
- Easy press creation mid-round
- Know where they stand at all times

**Pain Points:**
- Mental math during round is distracting
- Forgetting press details
- Disputes over who owes what

**Press! Solution:**
- Real-time game status
- One-tap press creation
- Automatic settlement calculation

### The Social Golfer (Casey)

**Profile:**
- 38, mid-handicap, plays for fun
- Enjoys the social aspect of golf
- Takes photos, shares stories

**Goals:**
- Stay connected with golf friends
- Share memorable moments
- Participate in games casually

**Pain Points:**
- Separate apps for scoring, photos, chat
- Missing out on banter after rounds
- Losing track of event photos

**Press! Solution:**
- Integrated feed and chat
- Photo sharing
- Event history

## Secondary Personas

### The Corporate Host (Dana)

**Profile:**
- 50, organizes company golf outings
- Runs 2-3 events per year
- Needs professional presentation

**Goals:**
- Professional-looking leaderboards
- Easy registration
- Calcutta for engagement

**Pain Points:**
- Limited time for setup
- Need scalable solution
- Collecting entry fees

**Press! Solution:**
- Event templates
- Share links for public leaderboards
- Stripe for registration fees

### The Newbie (Jordan)

**Profile:**
- 28, just started golfing
- Joined a work golf league
- Unfamiliar with golf betting games

**Goals:**
- Learn the games
- Participate without embarrassment
- Track improvement

**Pain Points:**
- Confusing terminology
- Fear of making mistakes
- Unsure of etiquette

**Press! Solution:**
- Simple, guided interface
- Built-in game explanations
- Low-stakes Alligator Teeth
