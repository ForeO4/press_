# Product Vision

## Mission

Press! makes golf events more fun and social by bringing the camaraderie of friendly competition to everyone's pocket.

## Vision Statement

The go-to app for golf groups who want to track scores, play games, and settle up without the hassle of spreadsheets, Venmo requests, or lost scorecards.

## Target Users

1. **Golf Buddy Groups** - Friends who play regularly
2. **Club Event Organizers** - Running tournaments and outings
3. **Corporate Golf Events** - Company outings with games

## Core Value Propositions

### 1. Simple Scoring
- Mobile-first scorecard
- Real-time updates
- Automatic leaderboard

### 2. Built-in Games
- Match play, Nassau, Skins
- Easy press creation
- Clear settlement

### 3. Fun Currency
- Alligator Teeth for betting
- No real money complications
- Just bragging rights

### 4. Social Features
- Event feed
- Group chat
- Photo sharing

## What We're NOT

- **Not a gambling app** - Alligator Teeth have no cash value
- **Not a handicap tracker** - No GHIN integration
- **Not a course finder** - Focus on events, not discovery
- **Not a tee time booker** - Just scoring and games

## Guiding Principles

1. **Fun First** - Games and social features are core
2. **Mobile Native** - Designed for on-course use
3. **Simple Over Complex** - Easy to learn, hard to outgrow
4. **Community Driven** - Built for golf groups
