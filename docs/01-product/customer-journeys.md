# Customer Journeys

Quick reference for Press! user types, use cases, and journey flows.

---

## Use Cases

| Use Case | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Casual Round** | Friends organize Saturday morning round with Nassau bets and skins |
| **Golf Trip** | 8 friends plan 3-day trip with daily games, team formats, running leaderboard |
| **Club League** | Weekly men's league with handicapped matches, season points, auto pairings |
| **Tournament** | Club championship with flights, gross/net scoring, live spectator leaderboard |
| **Guest Day** | Member invites 3 guests, sets up games with equitable handicap strokes |
| **Corporate Outing** | 72-player scramble with live scoring, proximity contests, instant results |

## Example Journey: Dave's Saturday Round

**Setup (Thursday)**
1. Dave opens Press, creates event at Pinehurst #4 for Saturday 9:30am
2. Selects games: $20 Nassau auto-2-down press, $5 skins with carryovers, $2 CTP on par 3s
3. Sends invite links via text to 3 regular buddies
4. Two join immediately; Dave sets up the third manually

**Day of Round**
5. Everyone checks in at course, sees their games with calculated handicap strokes per hole
6. Players enter scores hole-by-hole during round
7. Dave presses on hole 13 when 2-down on the back
8. App tracks all bets automatically

**Post-Round**
9. Walking off 18, everyone sees settlement screen:
   - Dave owes $8 to Mike
   - Dave collects $12 from Tom
   - Dave collects $3 from Jeff for skins pot
10. Players tap to record Venmo payments and close round
11. Round appears in group history with stats and memorable moments
